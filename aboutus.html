About Us

Just pets are a retail establishment that specializes in selling pets, pet-related products, and pet supplies.

We provide live animals as well as pet food, toys, accessories, grooming aids, and health care items. We also provide pet services including boarding, training, and grooming.

Unlike some other businesses, we are independently owned and run. Also, we offer an online store where clients can order goods and supplies and have them delivered right to their houses.

Our Mission

Our goal at Just Pets is to offer top-notch, secure, and healthful goods and services to improve the health and happiness of both pets and their owners. Our mission is to become the go-to source for all issues relating to pets by offering superior customer service and knowledge in pet care.
